Delete your account

Deleting your account is permanent. It removes your profile, lists, reviews, ratings, travel plans, and saved items. We are required by law to retain some records (e.g., financial transactions) for the legally required period. We may retain anonymized or aggregated data that can no longer be associated with you.

To protect you, deletion requires you to type your email address to confirm.
