What Zo2y does

Zo2y is a culture and lifestyle tracker where people save titles, build lists, post reviews, and manage interests across movies, TV, anime, books, music, travel, sports, fashion, food, and cars.

How Google Sign-In is used

Google Sign-In is used only to create or access a Zo2y account. The app uses basic account identity details such as your name, email address, and profile image so you can sign in and personalize your profile.

Minimum access only

Zo2y does not request Gmail, Drive, Calendar, Contacts, Photos, YouTube, or other restricted Google data. It uses only the minimum Google account scopes required for authentication.
