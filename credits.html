credits

These are the external APIs, catalogs, and platform services currently used across zo2y for media data, travel data, caching, auth, analytics, and email delivery.

content sources

TMDB

Movie, TV, and anime metadata, posters, credits, and popularity feeds for movies, tv shows, anime, and their detail pages.

Google Books

Primary search and popular discovery source for books.

Open Library

Trending books, work metadata, and cover fallbacks for the books feed.

Spotify

Music discovery, catalog enrichment, album detail data, and current chart signals for music.

Apple / iTunes

Music search, track previews, and album popularity signals blended into the music feed.

IGDB + RAWG

Game catalog data, covers, descriptions, ratings, and release metadata for the games pages.

REST Countries

Country names, regions, capitals, flags, and map metadata for travel.

Wikimedia Commons

Scenic country photography used in travel cards, travel spotlight visuals, and country pages.

Wikimedia Pageviews

Recent Wikipedia article view counts used to rank current popular travel destinations.

platform services

Supabase

Authentication, profile data, lists, reviews, notifications, social graph data, and cached application records.

Resend

Transactional email delivery for auth and account email flows.

Vercel Analytics

Visitor analytics and performance measurement for page traffic.

Source availability can vary by provider rate limits, upstream outages, and local cache freshness. When one feed is unavailable, zo2y falls back to the next available provider where that page supports one.
