Cloud Kitchen

A cloud kitchen is a delivery-only restaurant that operates without a physical dining space.

  • Delivery only - no dine-in option
  • Optimized for online orders
  • Often operates multiple brands from one kitchen
  • Lower overhead costs

These restaurants focus on quality food optimized for delivery.
