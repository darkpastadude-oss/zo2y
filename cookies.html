Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Zo2y uses cookies and similar technologies on the Zo2y website and progressive web app. It is part of our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They let the site recognize your device between page loads and across visits. "Local storage" and "session storage" work similarly but are stored by your browser rather than set by servers.

We also use related technologies like web storage, IndexedDB, service workers, and pixels. Where this policy refers to "cookies", it covers all of them.

2. What We Use Cookies For

  • To keep you signed in across pages.
  • To remember theme and accessibility preferences.
  • To measure site performance (only if you opt in).
  • To detect and prevent abuse.
  • To deliver the installable progressive web app to your device.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Zo2y itself does not set advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. The third-party services we use may set their own cookies on their domains. Those are governed by each provider's own cookie policy:

  • Google (Sign-In, Fonts)
  • Cloudflare CDN
  • jsDelivr
  • Supabase
  • Resend

These cookies are set when your browser requests a resource from their domains (e.g., loading a font from fonts.googleapis.com). You can block them with browser settings, but the corresponding feature may not work.

6. Manage Your Preferences

You can change your cookie choices at any time from the cookie consent banner.

You can also delete Zo2y data and start over using the clear-auth page, which removes local storage, cookies, service workers, and cached data.

7. Browser Controls

Most browsers let you:

  • See what cookies are set and delete them.
  • Block third-party cookies.
  • Block all cookies (this will break sign-in).
  • Clear cookies automatically when you close the browser.
  • Enable "Do Not Track" or "Global Privacy Control" (we honor GPC).

Browser help pages:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
