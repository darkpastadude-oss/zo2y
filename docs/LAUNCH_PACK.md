# Zo2y Launch Pack

## Core positioning
- Zo2y is the private-first taste map for people who bounce between movies, TV, anime, books, music, travel, sports, food, fashion, and cars.
- It combines saves, custom lists, reviews, and profiles in one account instead of splitting taste across niche apps.
- The hook: one place to track what you love across multiple categories, then share that identity with friends.

## One-line pitch
Zo2y is a Letterboxd-style profile for all your interests, not just one category.

## Short app description
Track favorites, build custom lists, write reviews, and follow real taste across movies, TV, anime, books, music, travel, sports, food, fashion, and cars.

## First 1000 users checklist
- Post the main launch post on X and pin it.
- Share the Product Hunt page on launch day.
- Post in 3 to 5 communities where taste tracking or list-making is already normal.
- DM friends and creators who already make lists or recommendation posts.
- Ask every early user one question: what would make you come back tomorrow?
- Repost the clearest user profiles and list screenshots.
- Highlight the multi-category angle every time. That is the differentiator.
